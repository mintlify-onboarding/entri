## Announcing Entri Version 2.0 "Lina"

I'm pleased to announce the release of Entri 2.0 today, nicknamed Lina. Version 2.0 of Entri is a major step in offering users a better experience inside of the Entri modal.

What's new in Entri Version 2.0?

- Fully responsive components
- Mobile and tablet support
- Security and performance enhancements
- Faster load times
